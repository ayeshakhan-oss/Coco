PROJECT OVERVIEW
────────────────
We want to create a visually rich, illustrated social media asset
showing how Taleemabad improved its candidate rejection emails —
and the positive feedback we received as a result.

This is a TRANSFORMATION STORY, not a testimonials post.

Output needed:
  • 3–5 illustrated slides (LinkedIn carousel, square 1080x1080px)
  • 1 portrait PNG (WhatsApp group, 1080x1920px)


VISUAL STYLE REFERENCE
────────────────────────
Style: TED-Ed animated explainer infographic
Feel: Warm, illustrated, educational, approachable, human
References: TED-Ed YouTube thumbnails, Kurzgesagt-lite,
            Khan Academy explainer graphics

Key characteristics:
  - Illustrated cartoon characters (diverse, friendly, simple)
  - Warm earthy color palette
  - Bold section banner headers
  - Icon-driven visual storytelling
  - Speech/quote bubbles
  - Clean readable typography


COLOR PALETTE
─────────────
  Primary red/coral:  #E05A4E  (headers, banners)
  Warm cream/sand:    #F5EDD8  (backgrounds)
  Sky blue:           #4A8FD4  (accent, dividers)
  Sunshine yellow:    #FFE066  (highlights, pills)
  White:              #FFFFFF  (cards, bubbles)
  Dark text:          #2C2C2C
  Soft green:         #5CB85C  (positive accents)


TYPOGRAPHY
──────────
  Headlines: Fredoka One or similar rounded bold (playful, TED-Ed style)
  Body: Nunito or similar rounded sans-serif
  Labels: All caps, tight tracking
