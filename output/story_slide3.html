Value 01  ·  Courageous Conversations

We decided to say
the hard thing.

⚡
The risk we took
Telling a candidate their CV had gaps could easily go wrong. They could feel judged, push back, or leave with a worse impression of us.
🎯
What we chose instead
We named the gap directly and honestly. We acknowledged what was strong. We gave real direction — not platitudes.
🪞
The harder truth
A courageous conversation isn’t just saying the hard thing. It’s saying it in a way the other person can actually receive.
💚
Why it mattered
Because every candidate put real effort into applying. Silence was not a kindness. It was avoidance.
