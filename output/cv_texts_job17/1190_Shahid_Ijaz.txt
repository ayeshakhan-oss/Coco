Cross-Linguistic Influence: Pashto Speakers Learning English Phonology, Grammar, and Vocabulary 
Conducted a mixed-methods investigation into how Pashto as a mother tongue influences the acquisition of English as

Linguistics Analysis of John Keat Poem ‘Ode to Nightingale’ Through the Lens of Stylistics 
Performed a rigorous stylistic analysis of John Keats’ poetry utilizing corpus linguistics software to examine lexical
choices and sentence construction. The study explored multi-level stylistic devices—including phonological,
graphological, and semantic features—to demonstrate how poetic "style" reflects an author's unique mentality and
voice. This research contributes to the field of applied linguistics by providing a framework for beginning students to
understand and utilize software for corpus-based literary analysis.
